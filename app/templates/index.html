How it works

  • Video files → FFmpeg audio extraction
  • Audio resampled to 16 kHz mono
  • Sliding 15-second window with 1s overlap
  • Each chunk → SSL encoder → CTC head → greedy decode
  • Timestamps written to .srt

Model
